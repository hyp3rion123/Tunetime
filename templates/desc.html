What is TuneTime?

TuneTime is an application that allows you to create transitional playlists based on events listed on your Google Calendar. Simply select the date you wish to create a playlist for, choose a combination of events you want to include, select songs you want to start those events with, and choose a song you want to end the playlist with. TuneTime then calculates a smooth transition between the given songs based on a variety of characteristics such as song tempo, energy, and acousticness to create a tailored playlist on Spotify perfectly suiting your mood for you to play throughout your day.

Additionally, if a user wishes to use a more compact version of TuneTime, they are given the option to simply choose a start song, an end song, and how many songs they want in their playlist.
